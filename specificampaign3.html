Tomb of Horrors

Dungeons & Dragons (3th edition)

Saturdays at 4:00 PM (EST)

Last session on 23/3/2019

In an isolated corner of one of the world’s fallen kingdoms lies the last crypt and redoubt of the demilich Acererak. For countless generations, this legendary Tomb of Horrors has attracted the avaricious and the noble, as well as the foolish and the brave, with legends of great wealth and even greater evil to be undone. Now a new generation of heroes stands ready to delve into the tomb’s deadly madness - or to be destroyed like those who came before them.
