Help menu

Here you can consult some tutorials:
1.- How do I create a character?

In order to create a character you must go to the top right corner of the screen. Click on the dropdown menu that says "campaigns" and select the option "Create Campaign". Once there you must fulfill all the requirements and click submit.


2.- How do I create a campaign?

In order to create a campaign you must go to the top right corner of the screen. Click on the dropdown menu that says "campaigns" and select the option "Create character". Once there you must fulfill all the requirements and click submit.


3.- How can I join the chat of a campaign?

If you are navigating throught the web and you end up in one of the public campaings or your own created campaign just click on the "join" button that you see on the bottom rigth corner of the screen. Once clicked, you will already be at the chat room and on the right side you can switch between different areas in the "rooms" panel and you can see the players that are on that room.
