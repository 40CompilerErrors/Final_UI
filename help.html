Help menu

Here you can consult some tutorials:
1.- How do I create a character?

Go to the home page by either clicking "Home" on the top-right corner, or your username in the top left corner. There, click the "New" button. fill the form with your character's name, group in which it will be saved, and profile image before clicking "Submit" to save it.


2.- How do I create a campaign?

Go to the top-right corner of the screen. Click on "Campaigns" and then on "Create Campaign". Fill the form with the name of the campaign, the system you are using for it, the usual playing time, a description, and an image to represent it. Then click "Submit".


3.- How can I join a campaign?

To join a public campaign you must go to the campaign page, usually accessed by clicking on "Campaigns" on the top-right corner of the page, and then on "Search campaigns". Then, click any campaign that catches your eye. This will take you to the campaign page. There, click on the "Join" button on the bottom-right corner of the page.


4.- I'm in a campaign's chatroom. How do I change the room?

Once you are in a chatroom, you will see a "Rooms" menu either on the rightmost side of your page if you are on PC or a tablet, or bellow the chat input if you are on your phone. Click or press the name of any of the rooms, and the current room in your chat menu will change accordingly.


5.- How can I create a group to save characters in?

Currently you can't, but if you've made it here, the Help menu is intuitive enough!.
